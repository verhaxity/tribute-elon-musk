Elon Reeve Musk

The Real-Life Iron Man

Elon Reeve Musk Genius of the Century

Elon Musk is an inventor and an engineer. He is the owner of, among other things, Tesla Motors and SpaceX. Musk is a genius and will go down as one of the greatest engineers the world has ever seen. He is pushing the boundaries of science and technology with his ambitions in electric power and space. Like a true engineer, he has developed solutions for the problems of modern civilization. He has plans to solve our traffic problems with autonomous cars and tunnel networks. Perhaps more remarkable, he has plans to eliminate most of our fossil fuel dependence and switch to cleaner and more sustainable sources of energy.

Main Accomplishments

1995

December 10

Elon Musk along with his brother founded Zip2, an online business directory, used as an online alternative to the standard paper yellow pages.

1999

Elon Musk along with three other co-founders launched X.com, an online bank.

2000

Elon Musk's X.com merged with a software company called Confinity, creating PayPal

2002

Elon Musk founded the space exploration company SpaceX which focuses on commercial space travel and the colonization of Mars.

2002

Elon Musk founded the Musk Foundation which focuses on supporting renewable energy, pediatric research, and education.

2004

Elon Musk started leading one of his most popular roles in the brand Tesla, which he started as an investor but would later become CEO of the company in 2008.

2006

Some of Elon Musk's cousins started a solar energy company named SolarCity with Elon as their primary financial backer. This company would grow to become the leading residential solar installer in the U.S. by the mid-2010s.

2015

Elon Musk co-founded a non-profit organization called OpenAI with a for-profit artificial intelligence reaserch lab component. This company was made on the grounds to research AI and in Elon Musk's words: "develop friendly AI that supports humanity."

2016

Elon Musk co-founded an organization named Nueralink with the goal of working on "brain-machine interfaces."

2016

Elon Musk started a side-business as a subsidiary of SpaceX with the goal of dispelling city traffic via underground tunnels called the Boring Company.

2022

Elon Musk bought enough Twitter stock to be a majority shareholder for the company and made a move to buy the company by the end of April. While the deal was decided there is still speculation whether he will actually go through with it or not due to Musk wanting to back out on the grounds of Twitter having "too many bots." As of right now the deal is on hold but since the Elon Musk and Twitter made the deal, he might be made to purchase it through court.
